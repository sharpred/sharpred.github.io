Welcome

I am Paul Ryan, co-founder and director of Step Up Software, a UK mobile app development consultancy. I am a Chartered IT Professional. I specialise in mobile application development and API development. I use Flutter/Dart, Javascript/node.js and Golang as my preferred technology stack.

My main professional interests centre around Continous Integration/Continuous Development (CI/CD) of Mobile Applications and of the use of Containerisation and Functions as a Service to support such development.

This is my personal blog site, but my long term aim is to use the posts on this site as content for a book I am planning to write on Devops for Mobile Application Development.
